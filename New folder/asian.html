Japan Main Dish 45 mins Medium

Tonkotsu Ramen

Rich and creamy pork bone broth served with ramen noodles, chashu pork, soft-boiled egg, and various toppings.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups pork bones
  • 2 liters water
  • 1 onion, halved
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 inch ginger, sliced
  • 2 packs ramen noodles
  • 200g chashu pork
  • 2 soft-boiled eggs
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • Nori seaweed sheets

Instructions

  1. Blanch pork bones in boiling water for 10 minutes to remove impurities.
  2. Rinse bones and return to pot with fresh water. Simmer for 12 hours.
  3. Add onion, garlic, and ginger after 6 hours of simmering.
  4. Strain broth and season with salt and soy sauce to taste.
  5. Cook ramen noodles according to package instructions.
  6. Assemble bowls with noodles, broth, sliced chashu pork, halved soft-boiled eggs, green onions, and nori.
