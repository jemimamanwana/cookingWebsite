Our Story

How Cooking with Jay came to be

Our founder experimenting with flavors

From a Home Kitchen to a Global Community

Cooking with Jay began in 2015 as a personal blog documenting our founder's culinary adventures while traveling the world. What started as a way to share recipes with friends and family quickly grew into a passionate community of food lovers.

After living in 12 different countries and cooking with local chefs and home cooks, we realized that food is the universal language that connects us all. We created Cooking with Jay to break down cultural barriers through shared culinary experiences.

8 Years
120+ Countries Represented
500K+ Community Members

Our Mission

At Cooking with Jay, we believe that cooking is more than just preparing food—it's a way to explore cultures, create connections, and celebrate diversity.

We're committed to preserving authentic culinary traditions while making them accessible to home cooks everywhere. Our team tests every recipe to ensure it works in real kitchens with accessible ingredients.

"Food is the ingredient that binds us together. Every recipe tells a story, and we're honored to help share these stories with the world."
Alex Chen Founder & Head Chef

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Cultural Respect

We honor the origins of every dish and work directly with cultural experts to ensure authentic representation.

Sustainability

We promote seasonal cooking and responsible sourcing to reduce our culinary footprint.

Accessibility

Great food should be available to everyone. We adapt recipes for various dietary needs and budgets.

Education

We don't just share recipes—we teach techniques and the stories behind the dishes.

Community

Our platform connects food lovers worldwide to share knowledge and experiences.

Quality

Every recipe is thoroughly tested by our team to ensure delicious, reliable results.
