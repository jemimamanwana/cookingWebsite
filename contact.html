Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

We love featuring community recipes! Please email your recipe to submissions@globalgourmet.com with the following information:

  • Complete ingredient list with measurements
  • Step-by-step preparation instructions
  • High-quality photos (minimum 1200px wide)
  • Information about the recipe's cultural origins

Our editorial team will review your submission and get back to you within 2-3 weeks.

Yes! We offer both virtual and in-person cooking experiences:

  • Monthly Virtual Classes: Live interactive classes focusing on different cuisines ($25 per session)
  • In-Person Workshops: Held at our NYC test kitchen (check schedule for upcoming dates)
  • Private Group Events: Custom experiences for teams or special occasions

Sign up for our newsletter to receive announcements about new classes.

We appreciate your help in maintaining recipe accuracy. To report an issue:

  1. Navigate to the recipe page with the error
  2. Click the "Report Error" button below the recipe instructions
  3. Describe the issue in detail (ingredient amount, step clarification, etc.)

Our test kitchen will review the report and make corrections if needed. You'll receive an email when the update is complete.

We offer recipes for various dietary preferences:

  • Use our recipe filters to find vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, or dairy-free options
  • Each recipe includes dietary tags when applicable
  • Many recipes include substitution suggestions for different dietary needs

For severe allergies, please review ingredient lists carefully as we prepare recipes in a kitchen that handles all allergens.

Our content usage policy is as follows:

  • Personal Use: You may adapt recipes for home cooking without permission
  • Online Sharing: You may share one photo with clear credit and link back to the original recipe
  • Republishing: Full recipes cannot be republished without written permission
  • Commercial Use: Requires licensing agreement

For all usage questions, please contact our editorial team at content@globalgourmet.com.
